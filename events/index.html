Reading Wars

October 1st - October 31st

A competitive event, a dramatic clash between three teams over the title Reading Champions. For every 15 minutes of uninteruppted reading you complete, you earn 50 points for your team and a coin with which you can attack the other teams to plunder their points! At the end of the month, whoever has the most points wins! To learn more check out the official [Reading Wars](https://bookerycommunity.com/events/readingwars) page.

To take a look at events on a calendar or add them to your Google Calendar, check out our calendar!
